About Us Page

At College Ride Collaborative (CRC), we are committed to ensuring that college students have access to safe, reliable, and affordable transportation options. Our mission is to eliminate the stress of getting to class on time by providing seamless public transit services, helping students focus on their academic goals without worrying about how to get to school.

Guided by principles of safety, cost-efficiency, and sustainability, CRC strives to create a transportation solution that not only serves students but also strengthens community bonds and supports eco-friendly practices. We work toward raising awareness about the benefits of public transport, connecting students with affordable ride options, and fostering partnerships with services like Uber, Lyft, and city transit systems to expand access and convenience.

Our Goals:

  • Safety: Ensuring secure and dependable transportation for all students.
  • Cost-Efficiency: Making public transit affordable for every student.
  • Eco-Friendly: Promoting sustainable transportation to reduce our environmental impact.
  • Community Solidarity: Building a supportive network among students and transportation providers.
